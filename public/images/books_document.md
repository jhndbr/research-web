## About the Author

**Dr. Cristiano Trindade De Angelis** is an internationally recognized researcher and author specializing in:

- Knowledge Management
- Organizational Intelligence
- Public Administration Reform
- Cultural Intelligence
- Governmental Intelligence
- Social Participation and Governance

His work has been published in numerous academic journals across multiple continents and languages, contributing to the advancement of knowledge in public administration, organizational management, and social innovation.

### Academic Contributions
Dr. De Angelis has published over 40 research articles in peer-reviewed journals across:
- South America (Brazil, Chile, Argentina, Colombia, Ecuador, Bolivia)
- Europe (England, Portugal, France)
- Asia (India)
- Africa (Nigeria)
- North America (United States, Mexico)

His research focuses on improving public sector effectiveness through:
- Knowledge management practices
- Cultural intelligence development
- Social participation mechanisms
- Corruption reduction strategies
- Sustainable development approaches
